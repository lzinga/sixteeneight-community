## About

SixteenEight is a simple, elegant fasting tracker designed with your privacy in mind. Whether you're doing intermittent fasting, extended fasts, or any other fasting protocol, all your data stays on your device—no cloud syncing, no tracking, no data collection. Just you and your fasting journey.

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from the community! Whether you've found a bug or have an idea for a new feature, we'd love to hear from you.

### Reporting Bugs

Found a bug? We'd love to hear about it! 

**Where to Report:**
- [Create an issue](../../issues/new) here on GitHub
- Post in the [r/SixteenEight subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/SixteenEight/) (an issue will be created for tracking)

**Please include:**

- **Device**: (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S23, Pixel 8)
- **Android Version**: (e.g., Android 14)
- **App Version**: (found in Settings > About)
- **Description**: What happened?
- **Expected Behavior**: What should have happened?
- **Steps to Reproduce**: How can we recreate the issue?
- **Screenshots**: (if applicable)

### Feature Requests

Have an idea to make SixteenEight better? [Open a feature request](../../issues/new) and include:

- **Description**: What feature would you like?
- **Use Case**: Why would this be helpful?
- **Mockups/Examples**: (optional but appreciated)

## Privacy Note

**Your privacy matters.** SixteenEight is 100% private—all your data stays on your device. We don't track you, collect your data, or sync anything to the cloud.

The app will always work locally first. Any community or online features that may be added in the future will be completely optional and purely designed to support you on your fasting journey. You can use SixteenEight entirely offline without ever connecting to any online services.

> **Note**: When reporting issues, please don't include any personal health data. Generic descriptions work great!
